About AI.S

AI.S (AI Solution) is a technology company specializing in developing and implementing advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) solutions. Leveraging state-of-the-art models and algorithms, AI.S aims to address complex problems across various industries, delivering intelligent, efficient, and scalable systems that meet the evolving demands of the digital era.

Goods Detection

Goods Detection (GD) is an AI-powered quality control solution designed to automatically detect defects in goods, significantly reducing reliance on manual inspection and cutting labor costs

Vision

The vision of the Goods Detection project is to become a pioneering AI solution in the field of intelligent quality control and product recognition, contributing to the digital transformation of the agricultural and manufacturing industries.

Mission

The mission of this project is to develop a smart, accessible, and scalable AI solution that automates the quality inspection of goods, especially agricultural products, through advanced computer vision technology.
